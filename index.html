About

I’m a senior at Miami University studying Political Science, Philosophy, and Economics, focused on rigorous mathematical modeling in finance and economics. My current work uses Mean Field Games and filtering (e.g., Kalman/EKF) to study belief dynamics, mispricing, and sentiment feedback in equity markets, alongside empirical analysis using U.S. market data.
