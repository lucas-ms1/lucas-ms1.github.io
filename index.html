About

I’m a senior at Miami University studying Political Science and Philosophy with an Economics minor, focused on rigorous mathematical modeling in finance and economics. My current work uses Mean Field Games and filtering (e.g., Kalman/EKF) to study belief dynamics, mispricing, and sentiment feedback in equity markets, alongside empirical analysis using U.S. market data. I’m looking for research roles and graduate programs in quantitative economics/finance, stochastic control, and applied math.
